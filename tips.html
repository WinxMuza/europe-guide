Before the trip

  • Check the validity of travel documents.
  • Compare transport and accommodation options.
  • Prepare a realistic budget.
  • Save important addresses, tickets, and contact information.

Recommended planning process

  1. Choose the destination or route.
  2. Decide the dates and length of the stay.
  3. Book transport and accommodation.
  4. Prepare a list of priorities and places to visit.
  5. Leave some free time for unexpected changes.

Glossary

Itinerary
A plan describing the order of places, transport, and timing.
Season
A period of the year when a destination receives the highest number of visitors.
Budget travel
A style of travel focused on reducing costs while keeping the trip practical and enjoyable.
