Before the trip

  • Check travel documents in advance.
  • Compare transport and accommodation prices.
  • Prepare a simple budget.
  • Save tickets, addresses and emergency contacts.

Planning process

  1. Choose the destination or route.
  2. Set the dates and length of the stay.
  3. Book transport and accommodation.
  4. Prepare a list of places to visit.
  5. Leave some free time for changes.

Glossary

Itinerary
A travel plan with places, transport and time order.
Season
A period when a destination receives many visitors.
Budget travel
A style of travel focused on lower costs and practical planning.
