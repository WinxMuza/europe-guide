Welcome to Europe

Europe is a continent with historic cities, famous landmarks, sea coasts, mountains and many cultural traditions.

Travellers can combine museums, old town centres, local food and short train routes during one longer holiday or several city breaks.

"Europe offers history, culture and variety in a relatively small area."

Quick facts

  • Many visitors start with short city breaks.
  • The EU makes travelling easier in many regions.
  • Europe is suitable for culture, food and nature tourism.

Why people choose Europe

Visitors often choose Europe because distances are shorter, transport is dense and many famous landmarks can be reached in one trip.
