Popular countries

Italy

Italy is famous for ancient history, art, regional cuisine and cities such as Rome, Florence and Venice.

France

France combines museums, famous monuments, gastronomy and different regions from Paris to the coast.

Spain

Spain is known for sunny weather, colourful cities, festivals and long coastlines.

Austria

Austria offers elegant cities, mountain scenery, music and good public transport.
