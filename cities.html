European cities are often the first destination for travellers because they combine culture, museums, architecture, and accessible transport in one place.

City selection

  1. Paris – a city of boulevards, museums, and iconic monuments.
  2. Rome – a place where antiquity and modern life meet.
  3. Vienna – a capital of music, imperial architecture, and cafés.
  4. Prague – a city with a unique historical atmosphere.
  5. Barcelona – a Mediterranean city full of colour and creative architecture.

Urban tourism

City break

A city break is usually a short stay focused on landmarks, walking routes, and cultural experiences. It is especially popular among travellers who want to see a lot in a limited time.

Walkable centres

Good city destinations usually offer efficient public transport, a walkable centre, and a high concentration of museums, cafés, and public spaces.
