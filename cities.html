Famous cities

Paris

Paris is connected with museums, boulevards, cafés and famous monuments such as the Eiffel Tower.

Rome

Rome combines ancient history, churches, lively streets and one of the most recognisable city centres in Europe.

Vienna

Vienna is known for elegant architecture, classical music, cafés and well organised public transport.

Prague

Prague attracts visitors with historical buildings, bridges, old streets and a strong city-break atmosphere.

City breaks

City break is a short stay focused on walking routes, museums, food, public transport and a compact historic centre.

Popular city destinations usually offer good railway or airport connections and enough sights for two or three days.
