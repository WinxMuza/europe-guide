Famous cities

  1. Paris - a city of museums, boulevards and iconic monuments.
  2. Rome - a place where ancient history meets modern life.
  3. Vienna - an elegant capital with music and imperial architecture.
  4. Prague - a historic city with a strong atmosphere and old streets.
  5. Barcelona - a colourful Mediterranean city with unique architecture.

City breaks

A city break is a short stay focused on walking routes, landmarks, museums and local culture. It is popular among visitors who want to see a lot in a limited time.

Good city destinations usually offer efficient public transport, a walkable centre and many interesting places close to each other.
